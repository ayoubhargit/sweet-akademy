Available Training

PLC Programming (Beginner → Intermediate)

Duration: 7 Weeks (26 Hours)

Modules:

  • Introduction to Automation (Siemens)
  • PLC Hardware – S7-1200
  • FBD & Ladder Programming
  • Timers & Counters
  • Digital & Analog I/O
  • Practical Projects
  • HMI Integration
  • Final Project
  • Certification
After this course, you will understand the fundamentals of industrial automation, confidently program Siemens S7-1200 PLC using Ladder and FBD, configure digital and analog inputs/outputs, implement timers and counters, and develop complete practical automation projects with HMI integration.

Price: 1200 MAD
